# Chokepoint Atlas — Product Description

## Overview

Chokepoint Atlas is a research skill for turning broad AI narratives into structured, evidence-driven supply-chain analysis.

Most AI investing conversations stop at the obvious names: GPUs, hyperscalers, memory leaders.  
This skill goes lower in the stack and asks the harder question:

**If AI infrastructure keeps scaling, what physically breaks first?**

That shift in question changes everything.

Instead of asking an AI model:

- “Which stock should I buy?”

this skill asks:

- What is the actual deployed system?
- What are the critical components in its stack?
- Which component is hardest to replace?
- Which suppliers are capacity-constrained, qualification-gated, or structurally underfollowed?
- Where is the market confusing excitement with real bottlenecks?

## Product Positioning

This product is designed for:

- AI infrastructure researchers
- supply-chain-oriented investors
- agents that need a disciplined workflow instead of generic market commentary
- analysts who want to separate narrative from physical constraint

## Core Method

The method combines two public research styles:

### Serenity-style

- start from the machine
- break down the BOM
- find the narrowest choke point
- compare strategic importance to market cap

### Crux-style

- treat the industry as a stack
- map roles by layer
- separate leaders from disruptors
- weight execution certainty more heavily than story quality

## Workflow

### 1. Define the Supertrend

Identify the real demand wave:

- optical interconnect upgrade
- AI factory power buildout
- cooling density expansion
- advanced packaging throughput

### 2. Map the Stack

Build a layered system view from end demand to materials.

### 3. Identify the Bottleneck

Ask what fails first if demand doubles:

- materials
- qualification
- capacity
- thermal constraints
- yield
- geopolitical dependence

### 4. Validate With Evidence

Rank evidence by source quality:

- filings and transcripts
- official disclosures
- industry reporting
- market research
- social signals as weak clues only

### 5. Output a Direction First

The skill does not begin with stock names.  
It begins with a directional lane.

### 6. Offer Names Only After the Thesis Exists

Candidate names are grouped by:

- proven executors
- pure bottleneck suppliers
- second-order beneficiaries
- early optionality

## Why It Matters

AI did not make investing easy.  
It raised the ceiling on how much structured research a single person or agent can do.

The edge is no longer asking AI for a recommendation.  
The edge is using AI to map industrial systems faster, test weaker assumptions harder, and find where the real constraint still hides.

**Which small, boring, physical constraint will matter more than the loudest narrative?**
