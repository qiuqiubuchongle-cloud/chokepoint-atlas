# Infographic Copy

## Title

Choke Atlas

## Subtitle

From AI narrative  
to physical bottleneck  
to directional lane

## Block 1

### The Problem

Most AI research starts too high:

- GPUs
- hyperscalers
- capex headlines

It rarely asks:

**What breaks first if AI keeps scaling?**

## Block 2

### The Method

1. Define the supertrend  
2. Map the stack  
3. Find the narrowest bottleneck  
4. Validate with hard evidence  
5. Output the lane  
6. Only then name companies

## Block 3

### Two Research Lenses

**Serenity**

- narrow and deep
- BOM-first
- hunt second-order choke points

**Crux Capital**

- stack-aware
- role-based mapping
- execution-weighted discipline

## Block 4

### Evidence Hierarchy

Strongest

- filings
- earnings transcripts
- IR disclosures

Strong

- supplier changes
- design wins
- capacity expansion notices

Medium

- industry reporting
- market research

Weak

- social posts
- rumor threads

## Block 5

### Output Layers

L1 Direction  
L2 Watchlist  
L3 Underwrite

No ticker dump before thesis.

## Block 6

### Core Question

Which small, boring, physical constraint  
matters more than the loudest AI narrative?
